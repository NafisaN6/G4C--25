Pitch

Shinrin, a town once isolated from civilization and shaped through the guidance of nature for many generations, is slowly forgetting its own history! Now, it is up to you, the player, to reteach the townsfolk what it means to be connected with nature once again. With its captivating world, rich story, and emphasis on nature’s beauty, Shinrin offers a unique adventure where every decision shapes the town’s future.

Nurture Yourself With Nature

Learn how to be more connected with the Nature surrounding you every day!

My Why

I picked this theme because Nature has always been extremely important to me. I grew up around Nature, constantly exploring the environment around me. Even with the internet being such a big part of kids' lives, including mine, I’ve always gone back to Nature for peace and inspiration. This game is a way for me to share that love with others who might not understand it yet.
